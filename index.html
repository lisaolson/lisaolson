My Story

I was born in Tulare, California in 1994. I grew up on a farm working in the fields from a young age. My parents emphasized the importance of hard work and global perspective. By 17, I had travelled to 8 countries and done volunteer work in Republic of Georgia, India, Mexico, Thailand and Belize as well as local projects in the area.

In 2012, I graduated high school and decided there was nothing I loved more than music. I found a Leadership College that also taught Vocal Performance in Sydney, Australia. I attended Hillsong International Leadership College for one year. In 2014, I decided I did love music, but maybe not as a full-time career. I decided to pursue something Math/Science related and started taking classes in Colorado.

By May 2016, I had finished 2 years pursuing Math/Computer Science general ed when I discovered coding bootcamps. In August of 2016, I moved to San Francisco to attend a 12-week immersive coding bootcamp. I graduated in November of 2016 from General Assembly and spent the following 2 months honing in on my skillset and portfolio.

In January of 2017 I began my first tech job as the sole Web Developer for a startup in Sunnyvale. I finished my 12-week contract end of April and have since been spending my time studying and building. I'm exploring how I can combine my art and volunteer background with a tech job to build beautiful and creative web applications that can make a difference...
